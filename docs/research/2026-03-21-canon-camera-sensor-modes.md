## Contradictions

### C300 Mark II sensor dimensions
- **Source A**: VFX Camera Database says 24.6 x 13.8 mm
- **Source B**: B&H Photo / Canon specs say active image area is 24.4 x 13.5 mm
- **Likely explanation**: 24.6 x 13.8 mm may be total sensor photosites; 24.4 x 13.5 mm may be the active recording area at DCI 4K. This parallels the C300 Mark III where DCI uses 26.2 x 13.8 mm vs UHD using 24.6 x 13.8 mm.

### C200 4K DCI maximum frame rate
- **Source A**: Canon CNA says "4K/50P DCI Cinema RAW Light" and "10-bit at 50P" (suggesting PAL markets cap at 50p)
- **Source B**: Canon Rumors and B&H say "4K DCI RAW up to 60P"
- **Likely explanation**: Both are correct -- 50p in PAL regions, 59.94p in NTSC regions. The max is 60fps (59.94p).

### 5D Mark IV 4K crop factor
- **Source A**: Initial release: 1.74x crop factor (pixel-for-pixel readout from 30.4MP sensor)
- **Source B**: Post-firmware update: 1.27x crop for DCI / 1.29x for UHD (oversampled from larger area)
- **Likely explanation**: Firmware update changed the behavior. Both are accurate for their respective firmware versions. The updated (1.27x) is the current behavior.
