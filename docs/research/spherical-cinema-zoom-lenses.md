## Contradictions

### Angenieux Optimo Ultra Compact Sensor Coverage
- **DZOFilm 18-35mm vs rest of Catta Ace image circle**: The 18-35mm covers 43.5mm while the 35-80mm and 70-135mm cover 46.5mm. This is confirmed by the manufacturer and not actually contradictory -- just worth noting the 18-35mm has a slightly smaller image circle.

### Fujinon ZK Series Sensor Coverage Description
- Fujifilm describes the ZK Cabrio series as "Super 35mm large format coverage" which is ambiguous. Some sources describe the ZK25-300mm as having 46.3mm coverage (matching Premista large format), while the shorter zooms are standard S35. The 25-300mm may have larger coverage than the others.
- Source: [Fujifilm ZK page](https://www.fujifilm.com/us/en/business/cine-and-broadcast/cine-lens/zk) vs [Hot Rod Cameras](https://hotrodcameras.com/products/fujinon-zk25-300mm-t3-5-to-t3-85-cabrio-premier-lens-pl-mount)

### Sigma Cine 18-35mm Sensor Coverage
- Sigma describes the 18-35mm T2 as "designed and optimized for Super 35 / APS-C format cinematography" but the "FF" designation in the product name on some pages suggests potential full-frame coverage. The actual image circle appears to be S35/APS-C.
- Source: [Sigma Global](https://www.sigma-global.com/en/cine-lenses/high-speed-zoom/18_35_2/) vs [Sigma USA](https://www.sigmaphoto.com/18-35mm-t2)
