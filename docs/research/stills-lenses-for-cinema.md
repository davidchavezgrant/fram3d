## Contradictions

### Sigma Art 24mm f/1.4 DG DN Minimum Focus Distance
- **Some sources report 21cm, others report 25cm**
  - Sigma official page and OpticalLimits: 25cm
  - CameraLabs and DustinAbbott: 21cm
  - Likely the 21cm figure is MF override, 25cm is AF minimum

### Canon RF 50mm f/1.2L vs RF 50mm f/1.4L VCM - Minimum Focus Distance
- Both report 0.40m minimum focus distance, confirmed by multiple sources. No contradiction - they happen to share this spec despite very different optical designs.

### Sigma Art 20mm f/1.4 DG DN Weight
- Weight not consistently reported across sources. Estimated at ~630g based on similar DG DN Art primes but not confirmed from Sigma's spec sheet in the retrieved content.

### Sony FE 85mm f/1.4 GM vs GM II - Focus Distance
- GM I: 0.80m
- GM II: 0.85m (AF) / 0.80m (MF)
- Slight difference due to redesigned AF system. Not a true contradiction.
